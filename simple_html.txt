<a name="PROJECTS"></a>
<font size="+2"><U>Projects</U></font>

<p>
Most weeks, we will have a programming project; the projects will vary, based
on the material we're covering in class.

<p>
Most projects will be due on Friday, at 5pm.

<p>
Each student is allowed 3 Late Days, which give 24 hours of grace on the due
date of a project.  You may only use a <b>single</b> late day per project (no
doubling).  Late Days cannot be used on the Pre-Project or the last project -
and there may be special projects through the semester which are also
restricted.
